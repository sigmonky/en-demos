The Echo Nest Unplugged

This web app creates playlists of acoustic tracks for Spotify using the Echo Nest Playlist API. Type in the name of an artist, and we'll make a playlist of mostly acoustic songs by that and similar artists.

Seed artist:
# Artist Title Acousticness

This app relies on the acoustic song_type that is calculated for millions of songs. For more information on using the acoustic attribute see this blog post: New audio attribute - Acousticness. Be sure to check out the sister app: The Echo Nest Plugged-in.
